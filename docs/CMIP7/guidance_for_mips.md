# CMIP7 Participation Guidance for MIPs

!!! tip "Documentation in progress"

    This section is in development and will be updated


The [WCRP CMIP7 webpage](https://wcrp-cmip.org/cmip-phases/cmip7/) provides an overview of CMIP7, including community MIPs (Model Intercomparison Projects) and a MIP registration form.

General guidance on setting up a MIP is provided in [Appendix B of Dunne et al. (2025)](https://gmd.copernicus.org/articles/18/6671/2025/#section7).

Guidance on naming MIP experiments is [available here](https://zenodo.org/records/14929769).
